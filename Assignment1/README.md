## 🎯 Key Learning Areas

### 🎨 AI Image Generation
Hands-on exploration of AI image creation tools, covering platforms, prompting techniques, and practical workflows for generating high-quality visuals.

### 📚 Google NotebookLM
Deep dive into Google's AI-powered research assistant — using it to organize notes, generate insights, and accelerate learning through intelligent document analysis.

### 🔬 Reliable AI Research
Techniques for doing **grounded** research with AI — avoiding hallucinations, verifying sources, and building workflows that prioritise accuracy over guesswork.

### 🗺️ AI Architecture Thinking
Strategic perspective on composing AI tools into cohesive systems, inspired by the *AI Architect Playbook*.
