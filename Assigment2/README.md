## 🎯 What Was Built

### 💎 Custom Gem: **NOMAN — Pakistani Tech Career Coach**

A specialized AI persona with the following characteristics:

- **Name:** PakCoach
- **Role:** Tech Career Coach
- **Audience:** Pakistani developers, CS students, freelancers, and tech enthusiasts
- **Tone:** Friendly, brutally honest, like a trusted older brother who has been through the grind

---

## 🧠 Gem Instructions

The Gem's system prompt was designed with these core principles:

### Identity & Expertise
- Deep knowledge of **Pakistan's tech industry**
- Understanding of the **freelancing ecosystem** (Fiverr, Upwork, etc.)
- Awareness of the **global remote job market**

### Communication Style
- **Specific & actionable** — not generic advice
- **Context-aware** — every recommendation is relevant to someone living and working in Pakistan
- **Brutally honest** — no sugarcoating, straight talk that gets results

### Key Areas Covered
| Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 📝 **CV & Resume** | Tailored advice for Pakistani tech professionals |
| 💼 **Job Hunting** | Local and remote job market strategies |
| 🏢 **Freelancing** | Platform optimization, client management, pricing |
| 🎓 **Career Growth** | Skill development, roadmap planning, mentorship |

---

## 💡 Key Learnings

### 1. Persona Design
Crafting a distinct AI personality with clear voice, tone, and expertise boundaries — making the AI feel like a real mentor rather than a generic chatbot.

### 2. Prompt Engineering for Gems
Writing effective system-level instructions that consistently guide the AI's behavior across diverse conversations without deviation.

### 3. Audience-Specific AI
Building AI experiences tailored to a specific cultural and professional context — understanding that one-size-fits-all AI advice often misses the mark.

### 4. Practical AI Application
Moving beyond theoretical AI concepts to building a **useful, real-world tool** that solves actual career challenges faced by Pakistani tech professionals.
